Recommendations from Qwykr Tech

FontForge A powerful font editor for creating custom .otf or .ttf files for your unique Dravidian-inspired script.
Zim (Desktop Wiki) An offline, local wiki ideal for documenting the syntax, culture, and historical evolution of your language.
